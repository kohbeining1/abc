Week 5

Neopixel on Microbit

Tripping

New Switch Exploration: Response for tripping

LED to change microbit is shaked or rotated. These actions to the microbit mimics common actions of the shoe when it is worn. This helps us move forward in the idea to create an alarming media response if person wearing the shoe trips over.

Jumping

New Switch Exploration: Response for jumping

LED to change color when microbit falls from a height and a certain level of acceleration. This is to mimic the jump action when shoe is worn.
